Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common inquiries about our tours and services

What is the best time to visit Uganda?

Uganda can be visited year-round, but the best time for wildlife viewing is during the dry seasons (December to February and June to August). For gorilla trekking, these dry months are ideal as the trails are less muddy and hiking is easier.

How far in advance should I book a gorilla trekking tour?

We recommend booking gorilla trekking permits at least 6-12 months in advance, especially if you plan to travel during peak season (June to September). Permits are limited and in high demand, so early booking is essential to secure your preferred dates.

What vaccinations do I need for Uganda?

Yellow fever vaccination is mandatory for entry into Uganda. Other recommended vaccinations include hepatitis A and B, typhoid, tetanus, and meningitis. We also recommend malaria prophylaxis. Please consult with a travel health specialist before your trip.

Can you arrange custom itineraries?

Absolutely! We specialize in creating customized itineraries based on your interests, timeframe, and budget. Whether you're interested in wildlife, culture, adventure, or a mix of experiences, our team can design the perfect Uganda journey for you.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept bank transfers, credit cards (Visa, MasterCard), and PayPal. For gorilla permits and certain accommodations, we require a non-refundable deposit at the time of booking, with the balance due 60 days before your arrival.
