Your trusted travel partner since 2015

At Wonderlust, we're passionate about showcasing Uganda's breathtaking landscapes, diverse wildlife, and rich cultural heritage. Based in Makerere Kikoni, our team of experienced guides and travel experts craft personalized adventures that connect you with the heart and soul of East Africa.
