Our Story

How Wonderlust came to be

Founded in 2015 by a group of passionate travel enthusiasts, Wonderlust began with a simple mission: to showcase the incredible beauty and diversity of Uganda to the world. What started as a small operation with just two guides and a single vehicle has grown into one of the most respected tour operators in East Africa.

Our journey began in Makerere Kikoni, where our headquarters still stands today. Through dedication to exceptional service and authentic experiences, we've expanded our offerings while maintaining our commitment to responsible tourism and community development.

Today, Wonderlust is proud to have introduced thousands of travelers to the wonders of Uganda, from the misty mountains where gorillas roam to the vast savannahs teeming with wildlife.
